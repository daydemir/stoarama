Notes

How scheduling works

A recording must be active and within your paid quantity to capture. Pausing keeps the configuration but stops capture. Deleting cancels it and frees the seat.
The minimum gap between fires and the maximum clip length are validated server-side; the clip must be shorter than the gap between fires.
The schedule preview here is advisory. The server is the source of truth.
