Pricing

Pay for what you record.

Browsing the catalog is free. You pay only for two things: the hours your recordings are actively capturing, and where those clips are stored. No subscription, no minimum, no setup fee. Add a card, set up a recording, and get one bill a month for what you used.

$0.05 per hour of recording. This is each clock-hour in which a stream is actively capturing at least one clip. An hour that captures nothing is free.
Storage

Choose where your clips are kept.

Storage is a per-recording choice you make when you set one up. Managed storage is priced by the hour of footage you keep. Everything below is billed monthly, in arrears, for exactly the time each clip was held.

Option Price What it means
Managed storage, monthly $0.10 per hour of footage stored, per month Stoarama keeps your clips. You pay $0.10 to keep one hour of footage for a month, charged only for the time each clip is actually held. Delete a clip and its charge stops.
Managed storage, yearly $0.05 per hour of footage stored, per month (effective) Half the effective rate in exchange for a commitment: you prepay 12 months up front with guaranteed 12-month retention and no early-delete refund. Available now by email; there is no self-serve yearly picker yet.
Record to your NAS about $0.02 per stream, per month A small client on your NAS pulls each clip every 10 minutes and we then purge our copy, so we only ever hold about 10 minutes of footage per stream. That averages about 0.17 hours held, which at $0.10 per hour of footage per month is roughly 2 cents per stream per month. This price holds only while your NAS is up and pulling successfully. If it stalls, clips pile up in managed storage and bill at the normal $0.10 rate until it catches up.
Bring your own S3 bucket $0 storage from us We record straight into your own S3-compatible bucket (Cloudflare R2, Amazon S3, Backblaze B2, Wasabi, DigitalOcean Spaces, or any S3-compatible endpoint). You pay your storage provider directly and we charge only the $0.05 per hour of recording.
How billing works

One monthly bill, after the fact.

  • You add a card on file through Stripe. Adding a card does not charge you.
  • Charges are metered as you record and settled once a month, in arrears, for the month just ended.
  • An hour in which a recording captured nothing is free. No capture, no charge.
  • See your running cost and this month's estimate anytime on /account.
