FAQ

Common questions

What triggers a recording charge?

A clock-hour in which one of your streams captures at least one clip. Idle hours are not billed.

What if I forget to stop a recording?

It keeps recording on its schedule and billing at $0.05 per recording-hour. Pause or delete it on your recordings to stop the charge.

What happens if my NAS goes offline?

Clips wait in managed storage. Anything held more than 24h bills at $0.10 per hour of footage, per month, until your NAS catches up, then the charge stops.

Can I see a large bill coming?

Your running estimate for the month is on your account, updated as you record.

Is there a minimum or setup fee?

No. There is no minimum, no subscription, and no setup fee. You pay for the hours you record and the storage you use.

How do I get the yearly rate?

Email us to prepay a year of storage at $0.05 per hour of footage, per month. Clips kept 12 months.

Can I record YouTube streams?

Yes. YouTube blocks capture from datacenter servers, so recording runs through a small relay app installed on your own computer. It uses your YouTube login in Chrome to resolve the stream. Cookies never leave your machine. Set up a relay under Your computers in organization settings.

Does recording via my computer cost more?

No. Rate: $0.05 per recording-hour, the same as cloud capture.
